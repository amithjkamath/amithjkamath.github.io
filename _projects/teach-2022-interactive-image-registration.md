## What It Does

Manipulate affine transformation parameters with interactive sliders and see how rotation, translation, scaling, and shearing affect medical images in real-time.

**Key Features**: Real-time parameter control, grid overlays for spatial reference, medical imaging examples (CT, MRI, X-ray)

## Why It Matters

Image registration aligns images from different time points, modalities, or orientationsâ€”critical for medical imaging. This demo makes abstract transformation matrices tangible by connecting parameter adjustments directly to visual deformations.

## What You'll Learn

- Affine transformations and matrix representations
- How rotation, translation, scaling, and shearing compose
- Clinical applications: multi-modal fusion, longitudinal studies, surgical planning
- Foundation for non-rigid registration and deep learning methods
